# Adidas-U.S.-Sales-Division
# Project Overview
You have been engaged as a Data Analyst by Adidas’ U.S. Sales Division to assess their sales performance and uncover new business opportunities. The dataset includes detailed information on products, revenue, regions, retailers, and sales channels.

Your responsibility is to clean, analyze, and visualize the data using Microsoft Excel. The goal is to generate clear, actionable insights that will support management in making informed sales and marketing decisions. Adidas Sales Dataset https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/heemalichaudhari/adidas-sales-dataset?resource=download

# Data Dictionary
- Retailer: Represents the business or individual that sells Adidas products directly to consumers.
- Retailer ID: A unique identifier assigned to each retailer in the dataset.
- Invoice Date: The date when a particular invoice or sales transaction took place.
- Region: Refers to a specific geographical area or district where the sales activity or retail operations occur.
- State: Represents a specific administrative division or territory within a country.
- City: Refers to an urban area or municipality where the sales activity or retail operations are conducted.
- Product Category: Represents the classification or grouping of Adidas products.
- Price per Unit: The cost or price associated with a single unit of a product.
- Units Sold: The quantity or number of units of a particular product sold during a specific sales transaction.
- Total Sales: The overall revenue generated from the sales transactions.
- Operating Profit: The profit earned by the retailer from its normal business operations.
- Operating Margin: A financial metric that indicates the profitability and efficiency of a retailer's operations.
- Sales Method: The approach or channel used by the retailer to sell its products or services.

# Analytics & Insights 
- The combined sales for 2020 and 2021 amounted to $899.90M, generating a total profit of $332.13M. Across the two years, 2,478,861 units were sold at an average price of $45.22 per unit. The overall profit margin for this period stands at 36.91%.
- There was a significant surge in revenue from 2020 to 2021, with revenue increasing by 294%. This sharp rise is likely linked to the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic on 2020 sales. In 2020, total revenue was $182,080,675, while 2021 recorded a much higher figure of $717,821,450. The revenue trend shows a steady rise from Q1, peaking in Q3 before declining afterward, indicating that summer is the strongest sales period. Q3 achieved the highest quarterly revenue at $265,308,354. At the monthly level, July was the top-performing month with $95,480,694 in revenue. Fridays generated the highest revenue among all days of the week, totaling $146,683,099, suggesting that customers tend to make more purchases on Fridays in preparation for the weekend.
- The West region recorded the highest sales at $269.94M, while the Midwest region had the lowest performance with $135.80M in total sales. At the state level, New York led with $64.23M in sales, whereas Nebraska recorded the lowest at $5.93M.
- The in-store sales channel generated the highest revenue at $127.59M, suggesting that customers prefer purchasing products they can see and evaluate physically. In contrast, the outlet sales channel recorded the lowest revenue at $96.56M, indicating lower performance compared to other sales methods.
- Men’s Street Footwear is the top-performing product category, generating $208.83M in total sales. This strong performance highlights its high customer demand and its significant contribution to Adidas’ overall sales.

# Findings
- In-store sales were the most effective sales channel, generating $127.59M in revenue. Online sales performed lower at $107.99M, indicating a need to strengthen the digital sales experience and enhance targeted online marketing efforts.
- The West region recorded the highest total sales at $269.94M. Several states generated less than $8M in sales, suggesting the need for region-specific improvement strategies to tap into unmet market potential.
- Men’s Street Footwear was the top-selling product category, with total sales of $208.83M. This underscores the importance of understanding customer preferences and focusing marketing efforts on high-demand categories.

# Recommendations
- Optimize operations during peak summer months, given their strong contribution to annual revenue. This may include increasing staffing, launching seasonal promotions, and offering tailored product assortments.
- Enhance the online sales channel by improving platform usability and investing in targeted digital marketing campaigns to drive higher online engagement and conversions.
- Leverage the positive sales growth trend by increasing investment in marketing, product innovation, and customer engagement initiatives.
- Continuously monitor sales performance and track key performance indicators. Incorporate customer feedback and insights to refine marketing strategies and improve product offerings over time.
